How to Use & Care for Your Bamboo Toothbrush

ü™• First Use

  1. Rinse the brush head under warm water for 10-15 seconds
  2. Apply your favorite toothpaste (pea-sized amount)
  3. Brush using gentle circular motions for 2 minutes
  4. Rinse thoroughly and shake off excess water

üíß Daily Care

  • After Each Use: Rinse bristles thoroughly and shake off excess water
  • Storage: Store upright in a well-ventilated holder (bristles facing up)
  • Drying: Allow to air dry completely between uses
  • Avoid: Don't store in enclosed containers or airtight covers

üßº Deep Cleaning (Weekly)

  1. Soak bristles in hydrogen peroxide or antibacterial mouthwash for 5 minutes
  2. Rinse thoroughly with warm water
  3. Allow to air dry completely before next use
