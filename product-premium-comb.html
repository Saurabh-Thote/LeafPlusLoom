How to Care for Your Wooden Comb

Daily Cleaning

  1. Remove hair strands after each use
  2. Wipe with a soft, slightly damp cloth
  3. Dry immediately with a clean towel
  4. Store in a dry, ventilated area

Deep Cleaning (Weekly)

  1. Mix mild soap with lukewarm water
  2. Dip a soft brush and gently clean between teeth
  3. Rinse quickly under running water (do not soak)
  4. Pat dry and air dry completely before storage

Conditioning Your Comb

To maintain smoothness and prevent drying:

  • Apply a few drops of coconut oil or mineral oil monthly
  • Rub gently across the surface with a soft cloth
  • Let it absorb for 30 minutes
  • Wipe off excess oil before use
