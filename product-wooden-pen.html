Usage Tips & Care Instructions

How to Use Your Wooden Pen

  1. Remove the cap by gently pulling it away from the barrel
  2. For first use, scribble on scrap paper for 10-15 seconds to activate ink flow
  3. Hold the pen at a comfortable 45-60 degree angle for optimal writing
  4. After use, replace the cap to prevent ink from drying
  5. Store horizontally or with nib facing down for best performance

Replacing Ink Refills

  1. Unscrew the barrel from the grip section (turn counter-clockwise)
  2. Remove the empty refill cartridge
  3. Insert new Parker-style refill and push firmly until seated
  4. Screw the barrel back onto the grip section
  5. Test on paper to ensure proper ink flow

üí° Tip: Replacement refills are available in blue, black, and red ink colors.

Daily Care & Maintenance

  • Cleaning: Wipe the bamboo body with a soft, slightly damp cloth. Dry immediately.
  • Polishing: Apply a tiny amount of natural oil (coconut or mineral oil) every 2-3 months to maintain luster.
  • Storage: Keep in a pen case or drawer away from direct sunlight and extreme temperatures.
  • Cap Care: Always replace cap after use to protect the nib and prevent ink from drying.

  • ‚ùå Never soak the pen in water or any liquid

  • ‚ùå Avoid dropping or applying excessive pressure on the nib
  • ‚ùå Don't leave uncapped for long periods (ink may dry out)

Pro Tip: If ink flow becomes inconsistent, scribble gently on scrap paper in circular motions for 20-30 seconds. This usually resolves minor flow issues. With proper care, your Wooden Pen can last for years!
